Upper Morningside Mutual Aid Network

Hello. We are a network of neighbors living in upper Morningside Heights that have self-organized in order to support one another through the coronavirus pandemic.

We don't know how long this will last, or what our needs will be yet, but we know that we'll all be better off if we help each other through it. Most of our members live between Broadway and Riverside and 122nd and 125th, but we welcome neighbors from the surrounding areas as well.

This page exists to help neighbors get in touch, so we can help each other weather the current crisis. We plan to post resources here about how to stay healthy, how to support local businesses, and how to get involved with our community.


If you could use some help:

We have volunteers ready to help you as needed. Our volunteers can help by:

  • Picking up groceries, prescriptions, or other supplies on your behalf.
  • Assisting with chores, childcare, petcare, meal preparation, and other tasks.
  • Socializing virtually, to help all of us stay sane.
Please fill out this form or email morningsidemutualaid@gmail.com, and we will connect you to one of our volunteers.

We understand that you may be uncomfortable sharing personal information online. We are committed to data privacy and will only collect information necessary to provding assistance. Only the coordinator and person helping will have access to your information and your info will be deleted once the task is complete.


If you want to volunteer:

Glad to hear you'd like to help out! We have a Google Group with everyone who's involve. Please fill out this form, and email morningsidemutualaid@gmail.com if you have questions.


How to Support Local Businesses:

Small businesses are especially struggling due to the effects of COVID-19. If you are financially able to do so, please support nearby small businesses financially, so they can continue to serve the community after this crisis passes.

We've compiled some information about which local businesses are open and how best to support them. If you have more information, please add it to this spreadsheet. In addition, Columbia University is maintaining a webpage with a list local businesses in their buildings to support.
